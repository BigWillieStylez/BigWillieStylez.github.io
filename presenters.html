Meet  Our  Presenters

March

March (she/they) is a curious student of rope as a medium for power, vulnerability, pain, & release.

In her teachings March focuses on facilitating personal growth, cultivating relational resilience through enthusiastic consent, & striving for active effective communication.

In 2015 March began falling hard & fast down the rabbit hole. Through that long journey she ultimately realized she identifies as an ever-evolving rope enthusiast.

March can often be seen teaching & performing nationally & organizing events throughout her local community.

March has taught for many notable organizations such as Tethered Together, Harvard University, Chicago People of Color, Ropecraft Chicago, Carter Johnson Library, Wellesley College, New England Rope Education Network, Anomaly Kink, Rednawa Studio, Bondage Expo Dallas, & Dark Odyssey.

Mandy  Von  Strange

Mandy Von Strange is a rope alchemist, energy worker, & community educator. Through her teaching she weaves together energy work, ritual, & the alchemy of rope to craft a unique experience for each tie.

Her primary focus is on blending structured technique with the fluidity of energetic exchange. She approaches rope as a transformative medium — an alchemical fusion of body, spirit, & intention.Whether grounding & meditative or cathartic & intense, each session becomes a bespoke energetic transmutation.

Mandy Von Strange created The Bondage Lab of Tidewater, has taught beginner rope & self-tying classes throughout the Hampton Roads & Williamsburg Virginia areas, performed at MX Virginia Leather 2018, & was a presenter at Shibari VA in 2023.

Her work is rooted in safety, consent, & inclusivity. She strives to ensure all who engage with this art feel welcome, seen, & empowered.

LynnieBunnie

Lynn (she/her) is a pansexual switchy Kitten who has been exploring the kink & BDSM lifestyle for over a decade, with a more active role in her local community since 2017.

Lynn is deeply passionate about fostering safer & more inclusive kink spaces. She regularly contributes to the community by hosting munches, supporting educational, & volunteering as a dungeon &  play space monitor.

Her mission is rooted in creating spaces where consent, communication, & kink can thrive for everyone; regardless of identity, experience level, or dynamic.

Winter  Nawa

Winter Nawa (they / she) is a community organizer, rope performer, & rope educator.

Winter Nawa started the Manila Rope Group in the Philippines, helped set up the North Bay Rope Lab in California, & recently started the Mac Rope Brunch.
All of these endeavors included organizing munches, creating curriculums, making lesson plans, & teaching in group settings.
